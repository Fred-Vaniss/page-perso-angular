### Mél'égumes
#### Group project at Technocité

* **Year:** 2019
* **Technologies:** HTML, SASS, JavaScript
* **Duration:** 3 months

{{gallery:0}}

This project was completed as part of a UX Webdesign training in partnership with Design Innovation. The objective was to design and develop a website for our client Mélanie to promote her permaculture business, Mél'égumes.  

Since the client had no content or branding guidelines, our team of three created all visual and textual content.  

**My main role:**  
* **Front-End Integration:** HTML/CSS and SASS for a responsive layout.  
* **JavaScript Development:** animations and dynamic interactions.  
* **Collaboration:** working with the team to integrate graphical and textual content.  

This project allowed me to strengthen my front-end integration skills, web design abilities, and collaborative work on a complete project.
