### Heliostart
#### Made at ADEVO Solutions

* **Date**: November 2019
* **Technologies**: Wordpress, PHP, JavaScript
* **Duration**: 2 months

{{gallery:0}}

My first internship project consisted of designing and developing a website to promote and sell **Heliostart** products and services: a device that automates the operation of a sunbed via an Android app, as well as website creation services.  

The project was based on a heavily customized WordPress theme:  
* **Front-end**: extensive CSS modifications and integration of components in raw HTML.  
* **Design**: creation of multiple graphic elements (logo, homepage background, pictograms…).  
* **Back-end**: development of a **PHP shortcode** to synchronize prices with WooCommerce data.  
* **Interactive features**: use of **JavaScript** to calculate and display the order summary, and to dynamically update the add-to-cart links.  

This project allowed me to develop skills in integration, WordPress development, and web design, while working on a complete e-commerce functionality.
