### Isotrim
#### Made at ADEVO Solutions

* **Date**: January 2020
* **Technologies**: Wordpress, PHP, JavaScript
* Project that I've participated

{{gallery:0}}

On this project, I mainly contributed by adding new features to the existing website:  

* **Custom side panel**: fully developed in PHP and JavaScript, allowing the integration of various modules, including a form for customers to request a callback at their preferred time slot.  
* **Interactive timeline**: redesign of the *About* page timeline, now animated with automatic scrolling and navigation through side buttons.  
* **Lightbox**: integration of an image preview system on the *Our services* and *Our realizations* pages to better highlight the content.
